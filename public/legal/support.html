Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Select OOTD → Cancel Subscription.
I can't sign in / forgot my account
OOTD uses Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In. Simply tap the same button you used when you first created your account. There's no separate password to remember.
AI suggestions don't look right
Try uploading clearer photos of your clothing items with good lighting. The AI works best with front-facing, well-lit images of individual items.
How do I request a refund?
All purchases are processed by Apple. For refunds, go to reportaproblem.apple.com, find the purchase, and submit a refund request there.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Your wardrobe photos are stored on your device only. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
The app shows wrong weather or zodiac info
Go to Settings (Profile page) and verify your location and zodiac sign are set correctly. Weather data updates automatically based on your location.
